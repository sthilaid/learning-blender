
* Board
    - 8x8, black in lower left
    - square: ~> 6 cm

* Pieces
** King
   - 3.75 inch height -> 9.5 cm ~> 9.0cm
   - diameter should be 40-50% of height and 75-80% of square
     (diameter ~> 4.5cm ->square ~> 6.0cm)
** Pawn
   - 4 should fit in a square (size ~> 3.0cm)
